Welcome to the First Gift Card Trading Center

Sell. Make the most of your gift cards with CardSwapr. Just by taking a picture of a gift card, you can now sell it to friends, or anyone else in the world for free.

Buy. You're able to easily purchase a gift card from the app, in almost any denomination, quickly and easily. Thanks to CardSwapr, this feature and many others are right at your fingertips in ways they never have been before.
The Most Features. There are 4 main tabs in the CardSwapr app: my wallet, browse, add card, and account. Each of these provides the cleanest, simplest way possible for you to buy, sell, and trade gift cards with anyone in the world.
