Features

My Wallet

This is your home base.
This page shows you all your active gift cards.

  • On the left, you see the company name for all the cards you have in your wallet neatly organized in a stack.
  • On the right, you see the USD value for your gift card.
  • When you tap on a gift card in your wallet, you see the expanded view of options: use card and view offers.
  • Clicking Use Card will bring up a simple barcode that you can scan at any retailer!
  • Clicking on the View Offers page will show you who has made an offer on your gift card!

Find A Gift Card for Your Wallet in 10 seconds!

      1) Open CardSwapr
      2) Click the Browse tab
      3) Search the retailer, user, or friend from which you want to buy.
      4) Buy (or make an offer) and it is added to your wallet! Done!

Browse

Here you can find any gift card, user, or friend, just by searching.

  • First search the card you want, then buy or make an offer on it.
  • Finding what you want has never been easier!

    • Making an offer on someone else's gift card:

  • When you find someone's gift card you want, you have two options:
  • You can buy their card for the full price (paying $25 for a $25 gift card)
  • Or, you can make a lower offer (offering $18 for a $25 gift card).

    • You can pay 3 ways:

  • Cash (through a linked PayPal account)
  • Gift cards from your wallet ($20 GAP gift card for a $20 Macy's gift card)
  • BOTH: Offer a $20 GAP gift card & $10 cash (via PayPal) for a $30 Nordstrom's Card

Add Your Gift Cards to Your Wallet in 10 seconds!

      1) Open CardSwapr
      2) Click the Add Card tab
      3) Take a picture of your gift card and add in any retailer information.
      4) When the balance is verified, click Add to Wallet. Done!

Add Card

Unlike other apps, adding Gift Cards to your wallet couldn't be easier.

      1) Take a picture of your gift card's barcode
      2) Add any additional vendor info.
      3) Hit add to wallet. Done.

Then, you have two options from which to choose:
Instant Sell & Open to Trades.

    Instant Sell

  • This features gives you another way to quickly sell your card by adding a price that buyers see when they view your card. It's like the 'Buy it Now' price on eBay. If you don't want your card and don't mind losing a few dollars in value in exchange for an easier selling process, it's recommended that you turn on instant sell and add a price (Ex: $50 Gap gift card; Instant Sell Price: $45).

    • Open to Trades

  • By switching this feature on, you are essentially putting your card on the CardSwapr market. Your card will now show up in searches and on your profile, visible to the world. Or, leave it off and just store your cards in CardSwapr for in-store and online use only.)
