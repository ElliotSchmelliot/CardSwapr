Features

My Wallet

This is your home base.
This page shows you all your active gift cards.

  • On the left, you see the company name for all the cards you have in your wallet neatly organized in a stack.
  • On the right, you see the USD value for your gift card.
  • When you tap on a gift card in your wallet, you see the expanded view of options: use card and view offers.
  • Clicking Use Card will bring up a simple barcode that you can scan at any retailer!
  • Clicking on the View Offers page will show you who has made an offer on your gift card!

Add A Card to Your Wallet in 10 seconds!

      1) Open CardSwapr
      2) Click the Browse tab
      3) Search the retailer, user, or friend from which you want to buy.
      4) Buy (or make an offer) and it is added to your wallet! Done!

Browse

Here you can find any gift card, user, or friend, just by searching.

  • First search the card you want, then buy or make an offer on it.
  • Finding what you want has never been easier!

    • Making an offer on someone else's gift card:

  • When you find someone's gift card you want, you have two options:
  • You can buy their card for the full price (paying $25 for a $25 gift card)
  • Or, you can make a lower offer (offering $18 for a $25 gift card).

    • You can pay 3 ways:

  • Cash (through a linked PayPal account)
  • gift cards from your wallet ($20 GAP gift card for a $20 Macy's gift card)
  • OR you can do both (Offer $20 GAP gift card & $10 cash via PayPal for a $35 Nordstrom's Card)
