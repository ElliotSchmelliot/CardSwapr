Features

My Wallet

This is your home base.
This page shows you all your active gift cards.

  • On the left, you see the company name for all the cards you have in your wallet neatly organized in a stack.
  • On the right, you see the USD value for your gift card.
  • When you tap on a gift card in your wallet, you see the expanded view of options: use card and view offers.
  • Clicking Use Card will bring up a simple barcode that you can scan at any retailer!
  • Clicking on the View Offers page will show you who has made an offer on your gift card!
