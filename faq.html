We use the Gyft API to verify the balance off 200 different types of gift cards. Those can be viewed here. We check the balance when you add the card initially and when you try and sell or trade the card.

Do I have to link my PayPal account to use CardSwapr?

With version 1.0, yes. However, in the next major release, slated for Spring 2014, we want users to not have to link any accounts in order to trade gift cards. Although, in order to ensure safety for all CardSwapr users, we initially require you to have an active PayPal account linked.
